InAppPurchase Plugin Demo

The InAppPurchase plugin allows you to have users pay for items or subscriptions right from your app. These items will be paid for by the connected App Store account.

Custom plugins don't work in the simulator and the AppBuilder companion app, so inside Telerik AppBuilder choose Run - Build - iOS/Android - App package.
